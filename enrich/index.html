ENRICH
WHAT WE DO
ENRICH is a fellowship program designed to support and develop the future of Houston as the hub of life science commercialization. Your company can support a team of selected students to work on problems for your company. The students will gain valuable experience working for a healthcare startup. The company will benefit from experienced entrepreneurs overseeing a team of students. The fellowship requires a donation which allows Enventure to facilitate the fellowship program as well as support the other Enventure programming.
Every Enrich project is unique, but our teams generally consist of 2-4 fellows, a project manager, and an experienced project advisor. Each project engagement lasts approximately six weeks, require teams to reach predetermined milestones, and can encompass multiple aspects of life science business strategy. Below are examples of Enrich project focus areas:
Category Example
Market Research
Intellectual Property and Licensing
Technology Evaluation
Marketing Strategy
Competitive Landscape Analysis
Regulatory Strategy
User Surveys
